JACOB'S REPTILE INFO

This website was started to teach people how to take care of their reptiles the right way. Many times when people buy a reptile, they are at a pet store and decide to take it home without knowing a thing about it. They take it home, feed it the wrong foods, give it the wrong enclosure, give it the wrong lighting, and so on. Pet stores usually tell people the wrong information on their reptiles and sell them the wrong supplies. On this website, there are many specific, informative articles on reptiles. New articles are put on this site every 1-2 weeks. If you have any article suggestions or questions about your reptile, please e-mail contact@jacobsreptileinfo.com.
