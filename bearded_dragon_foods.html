Foods Bearded Dragons Can Eat

October 1 2020




It is important to feed your bearded dragon the right foods. Baby bearded dragons eat about 80% bugs and 20% vegetables. Adults eat 20% bugs and 80% vegetables.


You should keep a salad bowl in your tank for your lizard to eat throughout the day. Look at the chart below to see what vegetables your bearded dragon can eat.


You should also feed your bearded dragon live insects every day. If your bearded dragon is less than 10 months old, feed it insects 2-3 times a day. If it is older than 10 months, feed it insects once a day. Always dust the bugs in calcium powder before giving it to your lizard.
